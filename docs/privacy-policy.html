FluxChi (FocuX) 隐私政策

最后更新：2026 年 3 月 18 日  |  生效日期：2026 年 3 月 18 日

核心承诺

FluxChi 不收集、不上传、不共享任何个人数据。所有数据仅存储在您的设备本地。我们没有服务器，也没有任何第三方数据追踪。

1. 我们收集什么

FluxChi 通过蓝牙连接 WAVELETECH EMG 手环，在本地采集以下数据：

  • 肌电信号 (EMG) — 用于分析肌肉疲劳度和专注力续航
  • 训练记录 — 包括会话时长、续航分数、时间戳

这些数据完全存储在您的 iPhone 本地（使用 SwiftData），不会传输到任何外部服务器。

2. 蓝牙权限

FluxChi 请求蓝牙权限（NSBluetoothAlwaysUsageDescription）仅用于连接 WAVELETECH EMG 硬件设备。我们不会扫描或连接任何其他蓝牙设备。后台蓝牙（bluetooth-central）用于在训练期间保持数据连接。

3. 本地网络权限

FluxChi 请求本地网络权限（NSLocalNetworkUsageDescription）仅在开发调试阶段用于连接电脑端 EMG 数据服务器。此功能在正式使用中不是必需的。

4. App Group 数据共享

FluxChi 使用 App Group（group.com.fluxchi.app）在主 App 和桌面 Widget 之间共享摘要数据（如今日训练次数、平均续航分数）。此数据共享仅发生在您设备本地的 App 与 Widget Extension 之间，不涉及任何网络传输。

5. 数据存储与删除

  • 所有数据存储在 iPhone 本地 SwiftData 数据库中
  • 删除 App 将永久删除所有相关数据
  • 您可以在 App 内导出或删除训练记录

6. 第三方服务

FluxChi 不使用任何第三方分析、广告、追踪 SDK。不集成 Firebase、Google Analytics、Facebook SDK 或任何类似服务。

7. 儿童隐私

FluxChi 不面向 13 岁以下儿童。我们不会故意收集儿童的个人信息。

8. 政策更新

如隐私政策发生变更，我们会在此页面更新。重大变更将通过 App 内通知告知用户。

9. 联系我们

如有隐私相关问题，请联系：epwujiajun@icloud.com

FluxChi (FocuX) Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026  |  Effective: March 18, 2026

Core Commitment

FluxChi does not collect, upload, or share any personal data. All data is stored locally on your device. We have no servers and no third-party tracking.

1. What We Collect

FluxChi connects to the WAVELETECH EMG wristband via Bluetooth and processes the following data locally:

  • EMG signals — used to analyze muscle fatigue and focus stamina
  • Session records — including duration, stamina scores, and timestamps

All data is stored entirely on your iPhone (using SwiftData) and is never transmitted to any external server.

2. Bluetooth Permission

FluxChi requests Bluetooth permission solely to connect to the WAVELETECH EMG hardware. We do not scan or connect to any other Bluetooth devices. Background Bluetooth (bluetooth-central) is used to maintain the data connection during active sessions.

3. Local Network Permission

FluxChi requests local network access for development/debugging purposes to connect to a desktop EMG data server. This is not required for normal usage.

4. App Group Data Sharing

FluxChi uses an App Group (group.com.fluxchi.app) to share summary data (e.g., session count, average stamina) between the main app and home screen widgets. This sharing occurs entirely on-device between the app and its Widget Extension, with no network transmission involved.

5. Data Storage & Deletion

  • All data is stored in a local SwiftData database on your iPhone
  • Deleting the app permanently removes all associated data
  • You can export or delete session records within the app

6. Third-Party Services

FluxChi does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. No Firebase, Google Analytics, Facebook SDK, or similar services are integrated.

7. Children's Privacy

FluxChi is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Policy Updates

If this privacy policy changes, updates will be posted on this page. Significant changes will be communicated via in-app notifications.

9. Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries: epwujiajun@icloud.com
