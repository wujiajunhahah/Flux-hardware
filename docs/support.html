System Requirements

  • iOS 17.0 or later
  • iPhone with Bluetooth 5.0+
  • WAVELETECH EMG wristband (for EMG data collection)

FAQ

Q: How do I connect the EMG wristband?

Open FluxChi, go to the Dashboard, and tap the connection button. Make sure your WAVELETECH EMG wristband is powered on and within Bluetooth range. The app will automatically discover and connect to the device.

Q: Why can't the app find my device?

  • Ensure Bluetooth is enabled in Settings
  • Make sure the wristband is charged and powered on
  • Try restarting both the app and the wristband
  • Ensure you have granted Bluetooth permission to FluxChi

Q: How do I add home screen widgets?

Long-press on your home screen, tap the "+" button in the top-left corner, search for "FluxChi", and choose from small, medium, or large widget sizes.

Q: Is my data backed up?

All data is stored locally on your device. If your iPhone is backed up via iCloud or iTunes, your FluxChi data will be included in the backup. We do not provide cloud sync at this time.

Q: How do I delete my data?

You can delete individual session records within the app. To delete all data, simply uninstall FluxChi from your device.
